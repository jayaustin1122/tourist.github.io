Calaguas Islands

3 December 2021

Calaguas Island is a popular tourist destination nestled in the Municipality of Vinzons in Camarines Norte. Over the years, Calaguas beach has been a go-to place for those who want to get away from the hustle and bustle of the city. Its powdery bone-white sand and cerulean waters can still elicit the perfect island vibe. The most popular among the beaches are the Mahabang Buhangin beach, which is made up of a long stretch of powdery white sand, and the Balagbag Island.
